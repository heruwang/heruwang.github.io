Website: Wuhan United Orgnization

Tool: Illustrator, Figma

Role: Visual design, User Interface

Design Concept

Wuhan United is a non-profit organization, devoted to fight the coronavirus outbreaks, collecting donations and delivering them to Wuhan, China. My goal is to emphasis the purpose and methods of donation on the website as well as giving the user an easy access to the information they need, including tracking update, news release and about.

Typography

I mainly used font roboto and lato. They both have a style of modern and formal.
