Artist Statement

My objective is to embrace the beauty of the world, the power of nature, and the love of the world. To be inspired by hummanities, my art is made for love and peace.

Currently, I am attending San Jose State University, studying Digital&Media Art. I will be graduating with a BFA degree in 2022. I am passionate to explore life, and get to know people. People and nature will always be my inspiration. My dream is to make arts that bring the audience positivity and hope.
